About the University

Andhra University is a prestigious public university in Visakhapatnam, offering world-class education and research opportunities since 1926. It is known for its vibrant campus, academic excellence, and strong alumni network.

Campus Life

Andhra University campus spans over 400 acres with state-of-the-art infrastructure, hostels, sports facilities, and libraries. Annual fests, cultural programs, and tech symposiums create a lively environment.
